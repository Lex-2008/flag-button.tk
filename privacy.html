Privacy policy

When you make request to this site, the following information is saved in standard apache log:

  • Your IP
  • Date and time of your request
  • Hostname (website) which you were requesting
  • User-Agent string of your browser, which contains:
  • Name and version of your browser
  • Your operating system

All logs are stored for indefinite time. They are used to gather general statistics. I might also analyse them when I suspect fraudulent activity.
